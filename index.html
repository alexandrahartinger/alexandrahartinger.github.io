Create Joy Holiday Campaign

Celebrating light, emotion, and craftsmanship with David Yurman’s 2023 Holiday film.

Overview

David Yurman’s Holiday 2023 Campaign, “Create Joy,” evokes warmth, intimacy, and timeless luxury. As an art director, I helped craft a visual story blending light and emotion to capture the spirit of the holidays.

Vision & Approach

We embraced evocative storytelling and cinematic visuals to create a sense of wonder. Drawing inspiration from holiday traditions and the sparkle of DY’s signature designs, we set out to make a film that felt both modern and timeless.

Execution & Impact

The final campaign featured a 60-second hero film and stills showcased across global platforms, bringing the message of “Create Joy” to life and driving engagement with David Yurman’s heritage of elegance.
