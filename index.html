Celebrating light, emotion, and craftsmanship in David Yurman's iconic holiday film.

About This Project

This campaign was a collaboration with the David Yurman creative team, where I contributed as an art director guiding the aesthetic and visual storytelling of the holiday film, “Create Joy.” The project leaned into a luxurious, emotive approach to celebrate moments of giving and light.
